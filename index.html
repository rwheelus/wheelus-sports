Welcome!

Hi!

Welcome to my site documenting my computer algorithm rankings/ratings of teams across various sports. Right now I have College Football, College Basketball, NFL, and NBA. I do my best to update the various rankings the day after most games have been played for the week. While my ratings generate fairly quickly, and with the press of a button, I must still prompt page generation manually. Given that, and being a busy person who travels often, I may sometimes fall a little bit behind.

My interest in sports data science and computer algorithms really started during the summer of 2018, when, in my free time, I produced an algorithm to rank all 130 College Football FBS teams. I tested this algorithm using the entirety of the 2017 college football season, and tweaked during the live 2018 season to achieve more accurate results.

This site is currently a work in progress. I am migrating from my former wordpress site to GitHub due to the easier long-term maintenance. In the process, I am tweaking some algorithms and adding additional sports and leagues.
